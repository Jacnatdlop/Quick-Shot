Welcome to Quick Shot

Quick shot is a game where you can test or improve your hand eye coordination

Click on the red targets to score points. Clicking on blue targets will decrease your lives.

About Quick Shot

Quickshot came to be because I am a fan of fps shooters. These games require precise mouse movement and hand eye coordination. I have tried many aim trainers and I thought it would be nice to make a fun version of it.
